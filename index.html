The Jewel Difference

Why Patients Choose Us

At Jewel Dental, we believe every smile deserves to be reimagined — not just repaired. Whether you come to us with a chipped tooth or a lifetime of dental anxiety, our team approaches every patient with the same warmth and expertise.

Our Schaumburg office is designed around your comfort. From the moment you walk in, you'll notice the difference: a relaxed atmosphere, a warm multilingual team, and technology that makes your visit faster and more comfortable than a traditional dental office.

We invested in digital X-rays that use 70% less radiation, an on-site 3D CT scanner for precise implant planning, and a dedicated Relax Room for whitening treatments — complete with a Zen 3D massage chair. Because going to the dentist shouldn't feel like a chore.

Serving patients from Schaumburg, Elk Grove Village, Roselle, Hanover Park, and the greater Chicago northwest suburbs, we welcome families, seniors, and patients who haven't visited a dentist in years. No judgment — just great dental care with a team that speaks your language.

From routine cleanings and preventive care to full-mouth dental implant restorations, cosmetic veneers, and Invisalign — everything your family needs is available under one roof, on your schedule, with flexible evening and Saturday hours.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept new patients in Schaumburg?

Yes — we proudly welcome new patients and offer a $99 new patient special that includes digital X-rays, a comprehensive exam, oral cancer screening, and ultrasonic cleaning. Call (224) 333-1113 or request an appointment online.

How much do dental implants cost at Jewel Dental?

Implant pricing depends on how many teeth need replacing and which solution fits best — single implants, All-on-4 Teeth in a Day, Diamond Bridge, or Snap-on-4. We provide a personalized treatment plan and cost breakdown after your free consultation so there are no surprises.

What languages does the Jewel Dental team speak?

Our team is fluent in English, Spanish, Polish, Russian, and Ukrainian. We believe everyone deserves to feel heard and comfortable at the dentist, which is why our multilingual staff ensures you can discuss your care in the language you're most comfortable with.

What are your office hours?

We are open Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday from 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Saturday from 10:30 AM to 3:30 PM. Evening hours make it easy to schedule around work or school. Same-day emergency visits are available — call us and we'll fit you in.

Where is Jewel Dental located?

Jewel Dental is located at 1321 Tower Road, Suite B, Schaumburg, IL 60173 — conveniently accessible from Elk Grove Village, Roselle, Hanover Park, and the surrounding northwest Chicago suburbs. Ample parking is available on site.
